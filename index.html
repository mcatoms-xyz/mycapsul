Why I built this

MyCapsul started as a personal project. I was juggling too many things, dropping too many balls, and no productivity app actually fit the way my life works.

So I built an AI agent that knows my world and a dashboard that makes it visible. It changed how I manage my days, my money, my energy, my kids' schedules — everything.

I believe AI should be accessible to everyone, not just developers and power users. MyCapsul is my way of making that real. It's free because the goal isn't revenue — it's impact.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

What MyCapsul Collects

MyCapsul is a local-first application. Your personal data (schedules, finances, health notes, goals, etc.) is stored entirely on your computer in plain-text files. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on our servers.

Mailing List

If you choose to join our mailing list, we collect your email address solely to send you product updates. We use a third-party email service. You can unsubscribe at any time.

Website Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand how visitors interact with this website. We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising, and we do not sell or share any visitor data with third parties.

AI Conversations

When you use MyCapsul with Claude Desktop, your conversations are processed by Anthropic's Claude AI according to Anthropic's privacy policy. MyCapsul does not independently transmit your conversation data.

Your Data, Your Control

Delete your MyCapsul data at any time by deleting the folder on your computer. No accounts, no cloud, no hidden databases.
